OUR SPECIALS

  • Orangish fresh drink

    Fully orangish taste comes from the lots of orange pulps. Sweet and sour passion fruit is added, producing sunlight and summer flavors in each mouthful. The corners of your mouth are raised! There’s a beautiful arc shape on your mouth. Wear a CC smile everyday!

    Orangish fresh drink is filled up with abundant vitality. It is mingled with the refreshed aroma of slightly sweet tea. A perfect golden proportion is collected on the tip of tongue.
